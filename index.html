J.S Bach - Lute Suite in E minor, BWV 996: II Allemande



Dylan Kontos is a Sydney based guitarist and tutor, currently studying a Bachelor of Music / Education(Secondary) at the University of New South Wales. Dylan is in his 5th and final year of study. He has attained an AMEB Grade 7 qualification with a high distinction. He has studied under Dr. Josinaldo Costa for 3 years and has played in the UNSW Classical Guitar Ensemble for 4 years. Dylan continues to progress his solo and ensemble playing with '4-Bit Guitar', a classical guitar ensemble passionate about arranging video game music for the guitar.
