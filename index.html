J.S Bach - Lute Suite in E minor, BWV 996: II Allemande



The UNSW classical guitar collective is a group of skilled guitarists who specialize in a variety of styles ranging from classical to flamenco, bossa nova, and jazz. All members of the group are former or current students at the University of New South Wales and have over 50 years of combined experience. The collective performs regularly at local venues and events, showcasing their mastery of the guitar and their ability to bring a fresh perspective to traditional guitar music. Whether playing solo or in harmony with one another, the group's performances are a testament to the versatility and beauty of the classical guitar.
